Where is the cruise headed?

The cruise will leave New Orleans making stops in Cozumel, Harvest Caye, Roatán, and Costa Maya before returning to New Orleans.

Will I need my passport?

A passport is not required to go on the cruise. However, you will need your passport if you wish to leave the ship at any of the destinations. If you are interested in any of the off-ship excursions, you will want to make sure your passport is up-to-date. More information can be found here.

Can I bring a date?

You can bring anyone you like on the cruise with you! Just let us know when you RSVP.

Are kids welcome?

Kids are welcome on the cruise and will probably have the time of their lives, but if you're looking to have a vacation without them we won't tell ;) Just let us know when you RSVP!

What will the weather be like?

We can't predict exactly how the weather will treat us, but I think it's safe to assume that it will be delightfully warm and tropical at all our cruise destination ports! New Orleans is typically in the low to mid 60s in December, but it is not unheard of for it to get down to the 40s so keep that in mind whenever you pack.
